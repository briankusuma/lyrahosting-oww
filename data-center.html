LyraHosting Data Centers Are an Industry Favorite for a Reason

At LyraHosting, we pride ourselves on the close relationship we’ve built with our customers over the past decade. Our data centers are the core of our operations, so we’ve reinvested every penny into our facilities to ensure they’re the very best on the market. With enhanced security, connectivity, performance, and support, LyraHosting is an industry favorite.

Here are a few of the main benefits you’ll experience when partnering with LyraHosting services and putting your faith in our data centers.

Electrical Supply

Our data center features a state-of-the-art electrical infrastructure that ensures unparalleled reliability and efficiency. Fully compliant with NEN 1010 standards, our setup includes both 2N and N+1 configurations, guaranteeing continuous power supply even in the face of unexpected outages.

With a robust power capacity that supports up to 4×32 Amp per full rack, your servers are always powered to their maximum potential. This high power consumption capability ensures optimal performance for even the most demanding applications.

Industry-Standard Cooling

Maintaining the perfect environment for your equipment is our priority at the LyraHosting data center. Utilizing advanced refrigeration and data center cooling techniques, we ensure that our facilities operate at ideal temperatures at all times. Our indirect free cooling methods provide efficient temperature control, preventing any risk of overheating.

This meticulous attention to cooling ensures that your servers and other equipment remain in optimal condition, enhancing their longevity and performance.

Enterprise-Grade Security

At LyraHosting, we’re responsible for keeping your data safe – which is a task we do not take lightly. We employ the best practices in both physical and virtual security to protect your sensitive data from all external threats. Our data centers are fortified with advanced DDoS protection technology, safeguarding against cyber-attacks and ensuring uninterrupted service.

We implement stringent physical security measures, including surveillance and access controls, to prevent unauthorized entry. 24/7, your security is our priority.

Lightning-Fast Connectivity

Ensuring seamless and high-performance connectivity is at the heart of our data center ethos. We offer a variety of connectivity solutions, including IP Connect, Carrier Connect, and Exchange Connect, each designed to meet your specific needs. Our innovative data centers provide direct connections that enhance network performance and reliability. Whether you need fast internet access or direct connections to carriers and exchanges, our services ensure that your network infrastructure is always optimally connected.

Experience the benefits of our cutting-edge connectivity solutions and keep your operations running smoothly and efficiently.

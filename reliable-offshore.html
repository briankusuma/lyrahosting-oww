What Is Offshore Hosting?
Offshore hosting – also known as DMCA ignored hosting – is where you contract an IT provider to host your website and its servers in a location that is outside of your home country. By not operating from your country of origin, you’re able to surpass the need to follow local regulations and government-enforced content restrictions. Especially in terms of censorship or data access that you don’t want to abide by, offshore hosting is the most efficient way of gaining total digital freedom.

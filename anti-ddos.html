What Is DDoS Protection and How Does it Work
A DDoS attack is a malicious form of bot traffic that aims to impede the functioning of your website and make it impossible for customers to access it. DDoS protection provides cybersecurity defenses against these forms of malicious attacks, protecting your site and mitigating these attacks. DDoS protection works by identifying the attack signature of botted traffic and ensuring it cannot connect to your site.
